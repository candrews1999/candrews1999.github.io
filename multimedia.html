Language University Lyric Videos

While at Language University, a foreign language education company, I created karoke music videos for two songs from their Spanish for kids album Listos. Editing the videos in Premiere Pro, I employed key frames to display each song's lyrics in a karoke format that kept up with the pace of the song. The videos are posted on Language University's Youtube Channel as a fun resource to help children learn Spanish through song and repetition.

Photography from Cuba

In the summer of 2019, I had the opportunity to study photography abroad in Cuba. While there, I practiced street photography and portraits. In capturing the atmosphere and unique culture of Havana, I spoke with locals in my limited Spanish and heard some of their perspectives on their culture and country. At the end of the program, I created a photo essay about minimalism and how it may translate to everyday Cuban life.

Visual Hierarchy Compositions

With this project, I had to balance texture, text, and image in one piece. With the amount of elements in each composition, I was challenged to find a visual solution that felt unified and balanced. I practiced masking in Photoshop, my overall design workflow in Adobe Creative Cloud, and photography.

Never Let Me Go Cover Redesign

In an effort to capture the perspective and emotions of the protagonist of Kazuo Ishiguro's Never Let Me Go, I designed an alternate book cover. I created seven versions of this book cover, with weekly critques guiding each improved version. With the final cover, I hoped to translate visually the protagonist's helplessness in her yearning for the past and the people she has left behind.
